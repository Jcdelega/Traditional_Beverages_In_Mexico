Traditional beverages in Mexico

Mexico is known for its rich variety of traditional beverages, each with deep cultural roots. Tejuino, made from fermented corn dough, is a refreshing drink enjoyed with lime and salt. Tepache, crafted from fermented pineapple, offers a sweet and tangy flavor. Chilate, a blend of cocoa, rice, cinnamon, and sugar, is a beloved drink from southern Mexico, served chilled. These beverages showcase the creativity and heritage of Mexican cuisine, preserving flavors passed down through generations.
